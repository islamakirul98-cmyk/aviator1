How to Play Aviator Rocket

  1. Place Your Bet: Adjust your bet amount in one or both of the betting panels and click the green "BET" button before the round begins.
  2. Watch the Rocket Take Off: As the round starts, the rocket climbs high, and the multiplier increases exponentially, starting from 1.00x.
  3. Cash Out in Time: Press the orange "CASH OUT" button at any moment to secure your winning payout: your bet multiplied by the current flight multiplier.
  4. Avoid the Crash: If the rocket crashes ("FLEW AWAY") before you click Cash Out, you lose your bet. The crash point is determined by a random number generator before takeoff, making each flight completely unpredictable!
  5. Dual Betting & Auto Modes: Use the second betting panel to place a backup bet. Use the "Auto" tabs to configure automatic betting or automatic cashing out at a specified multiplier (e.g. 2.00x).
This is a free demo mode simulator. No real currency is used. You can refill your balance anytime by clicking the "Refill" button in the header.
